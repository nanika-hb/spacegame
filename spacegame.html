Cosmic Survival Adventure

SpaceGame thrusts players into intense interstellar combat scenarios with procedurally generated alien threats. Master next-gen weaponry, upgrade your starship systems, and survive relentless extraterrestrial assaults in this browser-based roguelite shooter.

Key Features

  • Dynamic space combat environments
  • 15+ upgradable spacecraft
  • Alien technology research tree
  • Galactic leaderboards
  • Randomized cosmic events

Controls & Gameplay

Use WASD for spacecraft maneuvering, mouse for aiming and shooting. Collect cosmic energy to upgrade weapons systems between waves. Each successful survival run unlocks permanent upgrades. Compete for top positions in the galactic survival rankings.
